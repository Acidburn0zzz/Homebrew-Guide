Troubleshooting

Something didn't work right? Here are some common problems with their solutions.

The Homebrew Menu doesn't show anything!

This is usually due to an archive metadata bit used by some OS's that Horizon simply can't deal with. Here's how to fix it:

  1. Launch into Hekate
  2. Select 'Tools > Unset archive bit (all sd files)'
    • This might take a while, be patient.

When launching Hekate after shutting down from Horizon, it shows a Hekate logo and then doesn't boot.

This is a feature designed for modchips but can be annoying for other users. A later version of Hekate will have the option to disable this functionality. You can find out more on the Github issue

If this page doesn't solve your problem, feel free to ask for help in the support channel on the SDFilesSwitched Discord.
